About

Zerotheory was born in a small fishing village off the coast of the Duspur Sea. He spent his youth learning the crafts of sailing and had planned for a life making a trade from seamanship.

One day the evil wizard Granamindor came and wrecked the whole place. It was a real mess. Zero was the sole survivor. After discovering Granamindor had a strange fetish for oddly shaped tree knots, Zero set a horrific trap that rid the land of this evil wizard once and for all.

Zero found a strange portal deep in the wizard's lab. Of course he goes in, ya know with his village being trashed and all. He arrived in this strange land called "Earth" where he currently resides.

Zerotheory Games is a one man operation striving to spread the love and joy of playing games with your friends back into the mainstream. And he loves coffee.
